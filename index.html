    § 04 · Audio Echo & Doubling
    Audio probe pending…
    How this test works
    The app samples ~15 segments across your VOD (about 10 seconds each) and runs autocorrelation on each sample. Autocorrelation compares the signal against delayed copies of itself — if there's a strong secondary peak between 50ms and 400ms of lag, that's a characteristic signature of echo or audio doubling (mic picking up your speakers, duplicated audio sources, monitor bleed). Subtle room reverb and very short delays (<40ms) won't be flagged — those are usually inaudible on stream anyway. If Twitch's CDN blocks audio fetching (CORS), this section will tell you and offer to analyze an uploaded local recording instead.
